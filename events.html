Connecting with Professionals: Jeff Delmerico (Senior Scientist from Microsoft) - Mixed Reality & Robotics

On Sunday, November 22nd, at 11 AM PST (7:00 PM GMT), Jeff Delmerico will give a talk on Robotics, Mixed Reality, and the merging of the real and virtual worlds. He will also answer any questions you may have. This opportunity is provided by FIRST FTC Team 18225 team.

Jeff has over 10 years of experience in research, focused primarily on robotic perception. This work spans a variety of domains (visual-inertial odometry, machine learning, semantic perception), sensor modalities (monocular, stereo, and depth cameras), and hardware platforms (ground robots, marine robots, and drones) but a common theme throughout these projects has been enabling novel behaviors in real-world environments through active perception.
