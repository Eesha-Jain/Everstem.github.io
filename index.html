Welcome to Everstem Education

A non-profit orgnization run by students for students

Everstem Education is dedicated to making STEM educational opportunities more accessible for all students.

Event

Everstem provides students stem education opportunities including hackathons, workshops and seminars. The goal is to connect students with professionals in tech industry and help them prepare for stem related careers.

Grant

Everstem provides grants for new Washington FIRST FLL & FTC teams, as well as provides mentorships to help establish new teams in FIRST programs.
